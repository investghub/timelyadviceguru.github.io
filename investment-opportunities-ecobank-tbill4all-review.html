Ecobank TBILL4ALL Investment

AUTHOR: Jay Kay

UPDATED: Sep 13, 2024

Hi folks, I bring to you today one of the most innovative ways to invest in Treasury Bills in Ghana and that is the Ecobank TBILL4ALL platform. Ecobank as you know are one of the leading banks in terms of innovation and this comes as no surprise.

Before we dive into all this, I would like to recommend you to check our article on Treasury Bills in Ghana. This is especially recommended if you are new to Treasury Bills investments. There, you can find detailed information on what Treasury Bills are, their features and what you stand to gain when you invest in them. You can also check the FAQ section, where common questions are answered appropriately.

Now, let us take a look at this nice and convenient piece of innovation by Ecobank.

What is Ecobank TBILL4ALL?

The Ecobank TBILL4ALL is an innovative service that enables anyone to invest in Government of Ghana Treasury Bills on their mobile phone. This service portrays a high level of convenience, flexibility and even privacy for investors and I really commend Ecobank for this initiative.


WHAT ARE THE PROS OF TBILL4ALL?

Convenience:-

  • Potential investors can invest at their own time, place and pace. There is no need to be present at a bank branch.

Low Investment Amount:-

  • Any investor can invest in TBILL4ALL with as low as GHS5. Some banks I know set this amount at GHS500.

Open To All:-

  • This service is open to all potential investors whether you have an account with Ecobank or not.

Ease of Monitoring:-

  • This service is very easy to monitor. You can monitor whatever goes on with your invested funds directly on your mobile phone.

Assured Privacy:-

  • When no one is even aware that you are investing in something, that's privacy 😀.


WHAT ARE THE CONS OF TBILL4ALL?

Limited Reach:-

  • The service currently collaborates with MTN Mobile Money. I believe more mobile money platforms should be engaged.

Vulnerability:-

  • Smooth operation of this service depends on the effectiveness of the mobile telecommunication network. If the network is bad, a simple dial code cannot work and this may be frustrating.

HOW DO ONE ACCESS TBILL4ALL?

First time users of the service must dial *770# on their mobile phone to register. After resgistration, the investor is granted access to the product features including Application, the Interest calculator, Rediscount as well as the Terms and Conditions of the service.

Basic functions available on the service include:

Interest calculator: This allows the prospective investor to have a fair idea of what his/ her investment will earn at maturity. Rates used are those of the previous week and are only indicative and subject to change.

Rediscount functionality: This allows customers to terminate their investments before maturity. Partial rediscount is currently unavailable on the service. This is to make the product even more user-friendly and less cumbersome.
