EARN CRYPTO REVIEW: PAIDWORK

AUTHOR: KOSH TAPPY

UPDATED: SEPT 15, 2024

This is TimelyAdviceGuru. We are all about giving advice on ways to earn money online to sustain your living standards and today I am going to show you one good way of doing that. I need you to fasten your seat belt and tarry along.

We are going to talk about Paidwork. This is an organization that is committed to helping people worldwide by giving them an opportunity to earn additional income remotely. In fulfilling this, they provide their users with opportunities to make money by playing games, watching videos, completing surveys and performing microtasks.



How Can You earn With Paidwork

You can earn money with Paidwork from the following options on their app or web. These options are the most popular and I advice you stick to them as a starter and move to others as you gain more experience.

Watching Videos

Potential earnings: 1-10 points per video.
Users can watch video ads to make passive income. The videos shown have a duration range from 30 to 60 seconds. This means that just within a half or full minute, you can earn money. After watching the entire duration of the video, you will get paid. There is a quota on the number of videos you can watch in a day.

Play Games

Potential earnings: $0.10-$50.00 per game.
Users can play games to earn money on Paidwork. All they need to do is install the game they like and complete a specific task in the game, for example: Reach a certain character level or unlock a feature. Be informed that when it comes to playing games, some tasks take several days to complete. But be rest assured that the more difficult the game task is, the more you earn money.

Complete Surveys

Potential earnings: $0.10-$5.00 per survey.
Users can earn money by filling out surveys. These surveys come in a whole wide array of interests can a user can choose. Be informed that when it comes to surveys, the longer the survey filled the much better you are paid. So look out for the surveys that are long and you are sure to make more money. To prevent abuse, surveys have time durations and also requests for personal data.

Microtasks

Potential earnings: $0.10-$200.00 per task.
Users can make money on Paidwork by undertaking specified tasks. Users have two options under the microtasks: firstly, undertake tasks such as joining social media groups, posting comments on social media platforms and follow social media groups. Secondly, is to complete free courses to gain access to various tasks. These courses offers the advantage of teachning you new skills completely for free whilst earning money.






Tips To Earn More On Paidwork

Take a good look at these tips if you really want to succeed in this environment. I have been there before where I almost gave up because of how frustrating I was becoming due to low earnings. Back then I did not know these tips. But now you know, free of charge 😀.
  • Watch video ads to the end. Be patient and let it end or else you will not get paid. No half-payment.
  • Be consistent in performing tasks. Set a target for e.g. 10 videos a day and stick to it. Again, be consistent.
  • Take quality screen shots of tasks completed and upload.
  • Do not try to cut corners in completing any of the tasks. The end result may not be too good.
  • Spread the word. Get more referrals. Encourage them to complete more tasks and you all earn together. More on this later.

Why Choose Paidwork?

  • Simple user interface making it easy to navigate app or web.
  • An organization with credibilty i.e. will definately pay when threshhold is met.
  • An effective support system for all payment issues.
  • Commitment to improving livelihoods.
